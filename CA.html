Christ’s Ambassadors (CA)
  • Departments
  • Christ’s Ambassadors (CA)

The CA has been established to encourage wholistic development of youths so that they will be able to effectively witness the gospel of Jesus Christ among young people. It also promotes development of God given talents among youths so that they can contribute to the establishment of the Kingdom of God. A CA Committee comprising a President, Secretary and Treasurer will systematically organise the activities of this department. Programs like group and solo songs , song reference, bible reference, bible quiz, action songs, bible messages, special programs , etc were organised by the CA programs each month.

President: Rev. K. P. Cherian

Secretary : Sis. Jeni Paul

Treasurer: Sis. Liby Babu

Timing: 11:30 AM to 1 PM, third Sunday of every month

Venue: AG Church hall, Pala
