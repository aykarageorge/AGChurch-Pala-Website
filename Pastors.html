About Rev. K. P. Cherian

Rev. K.P .Cherian is a well known orator, anointed preacher & teacher. After completing B. Com. Degree, he surrendered his life for full time work of the Lord. He has completed his graduation in theology from Bethel Bible college, Punalur and Master Degree in Theology (M.Div) from Southern Asia Bible college, Bangalore. He has ministered in various Assemblies of God churches in Kerala such as Kattode, Kodupunna, Cherpunkal, Champakkara, Vanmazhy, Alacode, Chengannur Town, Kuwait AG, Madamukku, Mala, Mathoor, Thrissur Town and Aluva Town. He also ministered as section presbyter in Chengannur and Thrissur sections. He also worked as a faculty in various Bible colleges such as Asian Bible college, Kottayam, Bethany Bible college, Vakathanam, Maranatha School of Evangelism, Kumbazha, NPWM Bible college, Mavelikera and BBC DTE Chengannur. He was the coordinator of BBC Punalur DTE. Now he is the Pastor of Pala Town AG church and Chairman of Church Tribunal, AG Malayalam District Council.

He hails from Kottayam, Kerala. His wife Mrs. Juby Cherian has also completed her Masters in Theology (M.Div) from SABC Bangalore and as a family they are serving in the kingdom of God. His children Dr.Thankam Bernice & Family, Emil C Philip, John C Philip. Grand Sons - Benjamin & Emmanuel.
