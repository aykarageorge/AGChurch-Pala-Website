Sunday School
  • Departments
  • Sunday School

This department has been established to promote the spiritual well-being of children from the age 4 to 18. In order to nurture a balanced spiritual growth of children. Under the guidance of Malayalam District Council, a curriculum is designed and Sunday School text books are published for each age group. A Sunday School Committee comprising a Director, Headmaster, Treasurer and Teachers will organise the activities of the Sunday School Department in the church. Dedicated and voluntary Sunday school teachers appointed by the Sunday school Committee teaches Word of God through different lessons in the textbook published by AGMDC.

Sundays School Classes:
  • Beginner
  • Primary
  • Intermediate
  • Seniors

Timing: Every Sunday 08:30 AM to 09:30 AM

Venue: AG Church hall, Pala
