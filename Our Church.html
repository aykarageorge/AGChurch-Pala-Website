Church Info

The Assemblies of God Church, Pala is affiliated to the Malayalam District Council of South India Assemblies of God and started as a unit of HMC in the year 1991. The regular activity of the Church includes Worship Service, Youth Meetings (CA), Sunday School, Women Missionary Council (WMC), Friday Fasting Prayer, Bible Classes etc… The ministry and leadership of many spirit-filled servants of God during the last three decades have enabled the Church grow in number and to become one of the leading Pentecostal churches in Pala. We welcome you to join us for a powerful and uplifting Praise and Worship experience, and life changing messages from anointed servants of God.
