Welcome to Our Church

At the Assemblies of God Church, we are a vibrant and diverse community of believers who come together to worship God, grow in faith, and serve our local communities. Our church is part of the larger Assemblies of God fellowship, which is a global network of churches committed to spreading the message of God's love and grace.

Our Church main activities

We invite you to join us for our weekly worship services where we come together to praise and worship God. Our services are filled with uplifting music, heartfelt prayers, and inspiring messages from the Bible. Whether you are new to faith or a seasoned believer, our services offer a warm and welcoming atmosphere for everyone.
