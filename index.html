Welcome to Our Church

At the Assemblies of God Church, we are a vibrant and diverse community of believers who come together to worship God, grow in faith, and serve our local communities. Our church is part of the larger Assemblies of God fellowship, which is a global network of churches committed to spreading the message of God's love and grace.

Praise the Lord
Assemblies of God Church, Pala, is a place to meet Jesus Christ your Saviour. God has been gracious and embraced us with his eternal love. So we warmly welcome all of you to Come and join with us.

Testimony

  • By: Dr. Jinu David Mathai

Lord gifted us a Church Hall and Parsonage complex near Pala town in 2018. We humble ourselves and praise God each day how the Lord blessed us abundantly.
