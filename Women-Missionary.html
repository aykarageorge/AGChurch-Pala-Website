Women's Missionary Council (WMC)
  • Departments
  • Women's Missionary Council

“Serving the Lord” is the motto of this department of women believers of the Assemblies of God. The WMC promotes fellowship among women believers of the church so that they can mutually support each other in times of need. The main activities of the WMC is spiritual meetings, witnessing to God’s love through Jesus Christ in the community and charitable works. A WMC Committee comprising a President, Secretary and Treasurer will organise the activities of this department.

Timing: Third week of every month (Wednesdays) 10 am to 1 pm

Venue: AG Church hall, Pala

President: Dr. Jaicy James
Secretary: Sr. Saly Philip
Treasurer: Sr. Saly Jimmy
